HOW IT
WORKS

01
🏛️
Organization Issues

Accredited organizations connect their wallet, upload credential data, and a smart contract auto-mints a unique ERC-721 NFT tied to the recipient.

02
⛓️
Minted On-Chain

Certificate metadata is hashed and pinned to IPFS. The NFT is permanently written to Ethereum — immutable, public, and decentralized forever.

03
📤
User Owns It

Recipients receive the NFT in their wallet alongside a shareable verification link and QR code — embeddable on LinkedIn and job portals.

04
✅
Anyone Verifies

Enter a token ID or hash — the smart contract returns full verified credential data in under 3 seconds. No account, no fees, no trust required.
